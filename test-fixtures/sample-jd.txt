VP of Community

We are looking for an experienced VP of Community to lead our global developer relations and community initiatives. You will be the face of our community and the internal voice of our users.

Responsibilities:
- Scale our developer community from 10k to 100k members globally.
- Design and implement ambassador programs, developer meetups, and our annual flagship conference.
- Act as the voice of the community, funneling feedback back to the product and engineering teams.
- Lead a team of community managers and technical evangelists.

Requirements:
- 7+ years in community management, developer relations, or similar roles.
- Proven track record of scaling technical communities.
- Experience organizing large-scale events and conferences.
- Strong cross-functional collaboration skills, particularly with product and marketing teams.
