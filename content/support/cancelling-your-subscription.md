# Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time — no phone calls, no forms.

## How to cancel

1. Go to **Account Settings** from the top navigation
2. Click **Manage Billing**
3. In the billing portal, select **Cancel Plan**
4. Confirm the cancellation

That's it. You'll receive a confirmation email from us.

## What happens after you cancel

- **Access continues** until the end of your current billing period. If you're on monthly billing and cancel halfway through the month, you keep Pro access for the rest of that month.
- **No further charges** — your card won't be billed again.
- **Your data stays** — your module library, generated resumes, and account remain intact. You move to the free plan, not a deleted account.
- **You can resubscribe anytime** from your account settings if you need Pro access again.

## Cancelling an annual plan

Annual plan cancellations work the same way — your access continues until the end of your annual billing period. We don't offer prorated refunds for unused months on annual plans, but your access won't cut off early.

## Having a problem?

If you're cancelling because something isn't working right, we'd like to know. Email us at info@modulehire.com before you go — there may be something we can fix.
