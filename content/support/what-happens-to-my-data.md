# What Happens to My Data If I Cancel?

Your data belongs to you. Cancelling your subscription doesn't delete your account or your content.

## What's preserved after cancellation

When you cancel Pro and move to the free plan:

- **Your module library** — all your parsed modules, jobs, and skills remain in your account, fully intact
- **Your generated resumes** — every resume you've generated is still accessible in your Applications history
- **Your profile information** — your name, contact info, and account settings are unchanged
- **Your base resume uploads** — your uploaded resume files stay in your account

Nothing is deleted when you cancel. You just lose access to Pro-only features.

## What changes on the free plan

After cancellation, your account moves to free plan limits:

- Resume generations are limited to 2 per month
- ATS Estimator shows score only (no full breakdown)
- Module editing is read-only

Your existing generated resumes are still viewable and downloadable — the limit only applies to new generations going forward.

## Deleting your account

If you want your data permanently deleted, email us at info@modulehire.com with the subject line "Delete my account." We'll permanently remove your account, module library, generated resumes, and all associated data within 7 business days and send you a confirmation.

## Resubscribing

If you resubscribe in the future, everything is exactly where you left it. Your module library and resume history are fully restored when you upgrade back to Pro.
