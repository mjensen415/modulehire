# How to Contact Support

We're a small team and we read everything.

## Email us

**info@modulehire.com**

This is the best way to reach us for anything — billing questions, technical issues, feedback, or anything else. We aim to respond within 1 business day.

When emailing about a technical issue, it helps to include:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- The browser and device you're using
- A screenshot if you have one

For billing questions or disputes, email info@modulehire.com with "Billing" in the subject line. We'll respond quickly.
