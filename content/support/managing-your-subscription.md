# Managing Your Subscription

You can view and manage your ModuleHire subscription from your account settings page.

## Viewing your current plan

Go to **Account Settings** from the top navigation. Your current plan is displayed at the top of the page — either Free or Pro, along with your billing cycle if you're on Pro.

## Upgrading to Pro

From your account settings page, click **Upgrade to Pro**. You'll be taken to the checkout page where you can choose:

- **Pro Monthly** — $19/month, billed monthly, cancel anytime
- **Pro Annual** — $99/year, billed once annually (saves ~57% vs monthly)

Single-purchase options are also available if you don't want a subscription:

- **Single resume** — $9, one tailored resume generation
- **5-pack** — $29, five resume generations

Enter your payment details and click **Subscribe**. Your account upgrades immediately.

## Changing your plan

To switch between monthly and annual billing, click **Manage Billing** in your account settings. This opens your billing portal where you can make changes to your subscription. Changes to billing cycle take effect at your next renewal date.

## Cancelling your subscription

To cancel, click **Manage Billing** in your account settings, then select **Cancel Plan** in the billing portal.

When you cancel:
- Your Pro access continues until the end of your current billing period
- You won't be charged again after cancellation
- Your module library and generated resumes are preserved — you just lose access to Pro features
- You can resubscribe at any time

## Updating your payment method

Click **Manage Billing** in your account settings to update your credit card or payment information. Changes apply to your next billing cycle.

## Downloading invoices

All invoices are available in your billing portal under the billing history section. Click **Manage Billing** from account settings to access them.
