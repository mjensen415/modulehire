# I'm Not Receiving Emails from ModuleHire

If you're not getting confirmation emails, password reset emails, or other messages from us, here's what to check.

## Check your spam or junk folder

ModuleHire emails come from **info@modulehire.com**. Some email providers filter these automatically. Search your spam or junk folder for "modulehire" and mark any emails you find as "not spam" to allow future messages through.

## Check your promotions tab

If you use Gmail, transactional emails sometimes land in the Promotions tab rather than your primary inbox. Check there and move any ModuleHire emails to your primary inbox to ensure future delivery.

## Make sure you used the right email address

Double-check that you signed up with the email address you're checking. You can verify which email is on your account by going to **Account Settings**.

## Add us to your contacts

Adding info@modulehire.com to your contacts can help ensure our emails reach your inbox going forward.

## Resend a confirmation email

If you're waiting for a sign-up confirmation email and it hasn't arrived:
1. Go to the ModuleHire sign-in page
2. Try signing in — if your email hasn't been confirmed, you'll see a prompt to resend the confirmation

## Password reset not arriving

If you requested a password reset and the email isn't showing up:
1. Wait 2–3 minutes — delivery can sometimes be delayed
2. Check spam/junk and promotions tabs
3. Request another reset — only the most recent link is valid

## Still not getting emails?

Email us directly at info@modulehire.com from a different email address and we'll help you get your account sorted out.
