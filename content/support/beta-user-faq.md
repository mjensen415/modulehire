# Beta User FAQ: Your Pro Access Explained

If you signed up during ModuleHire's private beta, your account has already been upgraded to Pro — at no charge. Here's what that means.

## Why am I on Pro for free?

You signed up when ModuleHire was in private beta and helped us build a better product. That matters. As a thank-you, your account is upgraded to Pro for 12 months from our public launch date, with no credit card required.

## How long does my free Pro access last?

Your complimentary Pro access lasts 12 months from our public launch. You can see your exact expiration date in **Account Settings**.

## Do I need to add a credit card?

No. Your Pro access is active and you won't be prompted for payment information until your complimentary period ends.

## What happens when my free Pro period ends?

About 30 days before your Pro access expires, you'll receive an email with a renewal offer — priced better than our standard public pricing, as a continued thank-you for being an early user.

At expiration, your account moves to the free plan unless you've added a payment method and subscribed. Nothing in your library or history is deleted.

## Do I have access to all Pro features?

Yes — full Pro access for the entire 12-month period. Unlimited resume generations, full ATS Estimator breakdown, module editing, and multiple base resume uploads.

## I was on the beta waitlist but never got an invite. Can I still get this?

If you requested access during the beta period but never received an invite, email us at info@modulehire.com and we'll make it right.

## Questions?

Email us at info@modulehire.com — we read everything.
